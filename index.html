VIM for Kids

How to Play

Your Adventure

You're a brave little Cursor exploring the magical land of Textland! Find keys, make friends, and open gates to reach new worlds!

Your Keys

Click any key to learn more!

How to Win

  1. Walk around and grab the letter keys you find
  2. Walk next to characters to hear what they say
  3. Get all keys and the gate will open!
  4. Step on the gate, press ESC, and off you go!

Super Tips

  • Some golden keys unlock secret doors!
  • The characters know things — talk to everyone!
  • Tap the game title to pick a different level
FAQ & Troubleshooting

Keys h/j/k/l don't work?
Click on the game first! If you have a browser extension like Vimium, turn it off for this page.

Can't move somewhere?
Water, walls, and trees block your path. Stick to grass, dirt, and bridges!

Where's the gate?
Keep exploring! Once you collect all the keys, the gate opens up.

How do I pick a level?
Click the "VIM FOR KIDS" title at the top — level buttons will appear!
