VIM for Kids

How to Play

Your Mission

You are a Cursor — a tiny hero in Textland! Explore zones, collect VIM keys, talk to characters, and unlock gates to advance.

Moving Around

h Left
j Down
k Up
l Right

Arrow keys work too!

How to Win

  1. Pick up VIM keys (letters on the ground)
  2. Talk to characters by walking next to them
  3. Collect all keys to unlock the gate
  4. Stand on the gate and press ESC to advance

Tips

  • Look for special keys that open secret doors
  • Characters give hints — listen!
  • Click the title to show/hide levels
FAQ & Troubleshooting

h/j/k/l don't work?
Click the game board first. Disable Vimium/VIM browser extensions for this page.

Stuck?
You can only walk on grass, dirt, path, and bridges. Water, walls, and trees block you.

Can't find the gate?
Explore the map! Collect all keys and the gate will open.

Switch levels?
Click "VIM FOR KIDS" title to show level buttons.
